First Bulgarian Kingdom

Timeline ↓

  • 681 Khan Asparuh (680 - 700) created the Bulgarian state with the capital Pliska
  • 716 Khan Tervel (701-721) helped the Byzantine emperor regain the throne and in return received the region of Zagore and the title of Caesar.
  • 811 Emperor Nicephorus I Genicus captured and burned the capital Pliska. The battle of the Verbish Pass, where the army of the Byzantine Emperor Nicephorus was defeated.
  • 815 30-year peace treaty with Byzantium. Administrative reform divides the country into 10 districts, which are ruled by boyars elected by the khan. He built roads, fortresses, palaces and rebuilt the burned capital Pliska.
  • 865 Bulgaria officially embraces Christianity and baptizes the people.
  • 870 An independent Bulgarian church was established, recognized by Constantinople.
  • 886 The students of Cyril and Methodius arrived in Pliska.
  • 893 The Bulgarian language has been declared the official state, literary and church language. The capital moved to Preslav.
  • 917 The Battle of the River Aheloy - King Simeon's army defeats the Byzantine army. Bulgaria stretches between three seas.
  • 927 40-year peace treaty with Byzantium. The Bulgarian kingdom was recognized by Byzantium, and the archbishop was proclaimed patriarch.
  • 1014 The battle near the village of Klyuch - the army of Tsar Samuel was defeated by the Byzantines, led by Basil II.
  • 1018 The capital Ohrid has been conquered. Bulgaria falls under Byzantine rule. End of the first Bulgarian kingdom.
