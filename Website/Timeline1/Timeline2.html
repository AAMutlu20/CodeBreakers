Second Bulgarian Kingdom

Timeline ↓

  • 1185 The uprising of the two brothers Asen and Petar broke out in the town of Tarnovo.
  • 1187 After the successful uprising of the two brothers, the beginning of the Second Bulgarian Kingdom was created.
  • 1205 Tsar Kaloyan (1197 - 1207) entered the battle with the Latins naer the capital Constantinople and defeated them.
  • 1230 Tsar Ivan Asen II (1218 - 1241) entered the battle with the Byzantines and defeated them near the village of Koklotnitsa.
  • 1393The Turks invaded Bulgaria and conquered the Tarnovo kingdom.
  • 1396 After 3 years, the Turks conquered the kingdom of Vidin, and this put an end to the Second Bulgarian Kingdom.
