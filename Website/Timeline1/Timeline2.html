Second Bulgarian Kingdom

Timeline ↓

  • 1185 Khan Asparuh (680 - 700) created the Bulgarian state with the capital Pliska
  • 1187 Khan Tervel (701-721) helped the Byzantine emperor regain the throne and in return received the region of Zagore and the title of Caesar.
  • 1205 Emperor Nicephorus I Genicus captured and burned the capital Pliska. The battle of the Verbish Pass, where the army of the Byzantine Emperor Nicephorus was defeated.
  • 1230 30-year peace treaty with Byzantium. Administrative reform divides the country into 10 districts, which are ruled by boyars elected by the khan. He built roads, fortresses, palaces and rebuilt the burned capital Pliska.
  • 1393 Bulgaria officially embraces Christianity and baptizes the people.
  • 1396 An independent Bulgarian church was established, recognized by Constantinople.
